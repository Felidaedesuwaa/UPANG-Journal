Information Technology Support

In this building you may find:

  • The central office for CSDL Information Technology Support, dedicated to assisting students, faculty, and staff with their IT needs.
  • Technical support staff ready to troubleshoot computer issues, network connectivity problems, and software-related inquiries.
  • Potentially a help desk or service counter where you can directly seek assistance.
  • Resources and information related to university IT services, policies, and best practices.

This crucial support service is strategically located to be easily accessible from various key areas on campus, including:

  • Office of the President
  • PTC Building
  • University Atrium

It is also situated near the Main Gate for easy access.
