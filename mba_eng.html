MBA ENGINEERING

In this building you may find:

  • Dedicated learning spaces for Engineering and Architecture students, fostering creativity and technical expertise.
  • Specialized laboratories for various engineering fields (e.g., civil, mechanical, electrical engineering labs) providing practical, hands-on experience.
  • Architecture studios and drafting rooms equipped for design work, model building, and computer-aided design.
  • Classrooms and lecture halls for theoretical instruction in engineering principles and architectural concepts.
  • Project rooms and collaborative workspaces encouraging teamwork and the development of innovative designs and solutions.

This MBA Engineering Building serves as the central hub for our future engineers and architects, conveniently located near:

  • The University Gym
  • The MBA Hall
  • University Gymnasium
