Riverside

In this building you may find:

  • Dedicated learning spaces for future healthcare professionals in Nursing, Medical Technology, and Pharmacy.
  • Specialized laboratories designed for hands-on training in each medical field (e.g., nursing simulation labs, medical technology analysis labs, pharmaceutical compounding areas).
  • Classrooms and lecture halls facilitating in-depth learning of medical sciences and practices.
  • Faculty offices and consultation rooms for professors and instructors from the Nursing, Medical Technology, and Pharmacy departments.
  • Student lounges and collaborative study areas for medical students to connect and learn together.

This Riverside Building is a central point for our medical students, conveniently located near:

  • The University Gym
  • The MBA Engineering Building
  • The MBA Hall
